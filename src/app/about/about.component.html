About Me

Hello! I'm Manu Kapolke, a passionate frontend developer from Erlangen, Germany. Captivated by the ever-evolving world of web development, I immensely enjoy building impressive and user-friendly web experiences.

With a background in mathematics, I hold a Ph.D. in the subject. This academic journey has proven to be a valuable asset for me as a Frontend Developer. Mathematics has honed my problem-solving skills, cultivated a keen attention to detail, and instilled in me a strong analytical mindset. These qualities have seamlessly translated into the world of web development, where precision and creative problem-solving are paramount. It's the perfect foundation for creating elegant and efficient frontend solutions. Schönheit des Abstrakten. Bei Programmierung: Schönheit von clean code.

During my academic pursuit, it became clear that my true passion lay in programming and software development. This realization marked a turning point in my life. The transition from mathematical proofs to coding logic felt natural on the one hand, yet like discovering the missing piece of the puzzle in my journey. Now, I'm fully immersed in the world of software development, constantly learning and pushing the boundaries of what's possible in web design and interactivity.
